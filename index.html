Tour & Destination

3 Days Jaljala Hills Trek

Every people know that live in Gandaki Province, Nepal, Jaljala is one of the historical and most attractive places for tourists, where national and international tourists trek there.

You can also visit the Annapurna base camp before visiting Jaljala hills.

Visiting Annapurna base camp is so interesting and fruitful for you. From there, you can get attraction to the natural beauty as well as experience the Nepali Gurung community culture like Rodhi. You can get a peaceful homestay that provides Nepali food.

Pokhara Tour

Pokhara is one of the famous tourist destinations of Nepal where many national and international tourists go to celebrate their vacations. Visitors can get many attractions such as natural beauty, a peaceful city, different cultures, languages, foods, and adventure tourism like bungee jumping and paragliding.

Parbat Kushma Bunjee Tour

Interested in an adventure travel? We can get the world's second tallest bungee jump in Kushma city. You can do bungee single or as a couple there. You can also experience the highest jeep flyer in there.

Baglung Kalika Temple

Baglung Kalika Temple is one of the popular historical temples in Gandaki Province where numerous people go for worship.

Shalija Village Trek

Shalija is a rural village which is near Jaljala Hill.
